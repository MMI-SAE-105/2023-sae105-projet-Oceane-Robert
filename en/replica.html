Kung Fu Panda : The art of living the present

Since its release in 2008, “Kung Fu Panda” has won over millions of viewers with one of the most valuable lessons of the film that conveys “yesterday is behind, tomorrow is mystery and today is a gift, that’s why we call the present” This phrase resonates throughout the film and becomes our common thread for the Po panda adventure.

Po, l'exemple vivant :

The main character Po, a jovial and clumsy panda, dreams of becoming a master of kung fu, despite an unusual appearance and lack of experience. However, Po fully embodies the replica by choosing to live the present moment. He continues to believe in himself after the failures of the past and the uncertainty of the future. Instead, it considers every day as an opportunity.

Au-delà de l'animation :

The film “Kung Fu Panda” goes beyond the limits of animation to show us that perseverance, self-confidence and determination to live in the present moment are the keys to success.

Le présent, un cadeaux précieux :

We too can tap into our inner potential to reach new heights. We discover every day is indeed a gift of life. The present allows us to grow, learn and approach our dreams. We can choose to live the present perfectly and make it a gift for the brighter future.

devenir le héros de notre histoire :

“Kung Fu Panda” reminds us of all that the present is a precious treasure to cherish. Thanks to Po, we understand that we can become the hero of our own story and that life is an adventure at every moment. So, let’s live every day as the gift that it is.

“On rencontre souvent sa destinée par les chemins qu’on a pris pour l’éviter.”

—Jean de la Fontaine, Fables
