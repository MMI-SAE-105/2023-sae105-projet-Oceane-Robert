Mark Obsorne et john Stevenson

We will delve into the magical world of animation by telling the story of the two brains who gave life to the film of “Kung Fu Panda” Mark Osborne and John Stevenson conquered the hearts of the whole world.

Mark Osborne

Mark Osborne is the brains behind the camera that helped bring Po’s fascinating story and journey to life. Osborne is a creative who has captured the essence of kung fu, comedy and unforgettable emotion.

He made sure that every scene was super animated, creating a world that transported viewers into an extraordinary adventure.

John Stevenson

John Stevenson brought true authenticity to epic combat scenes. Stevenson ensured that the characters' movements were fluid and realistic, while remaining true to kung fu techniques.

Stevenson also helped create dynamic and exciting combat sequences. The fighting scenes have become highlights of the film, captivating the audience at all ages.

The harmony of two spirits

Collaboration was essential to bring “Kung Fu Panda” to life. Their united harmonies have created a film that combines action, comedy and deep lessons.

Mark Osborne and John Stevenson followed their vision and worked as a team to create a film that touched the hearts of the world.

Mark Osborne and John Stevenson have the art of telling stories through animation. Their work has left a monumental mark on generations of all kinds. Their creativity and expertise have made it a must-see movie. We hope their story inspires you to follow your own dreams. So, get ready to travel through the epic world of “Kung Fu Panda” while enjoying the outstanding talents of the directors.
