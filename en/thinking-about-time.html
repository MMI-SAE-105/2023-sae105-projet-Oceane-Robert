Thinking about time

The thought of the time of André Comte-Sponville and his link with the reply “yesterday is behind, tomorrow is mystery and today is a gift, that’s why we call it the present”. This replica has a profound meaning that can help to better understand our relationship to time with the help of the book on time thinking.

The time in the literature

The book is a powerful way to explore complex ideas and the notion of time. The book addresses how we perceive, measure and live time. We have the opportunity to dive into history and discover different perspectives on time le temps.

The art of Kung Fu Panda and the philosophy of time

In Kung Fu Panda, this approach allows him to focus on his dreams and persevere on his journey to become a kung fu master.

This philosophy of time reminds us that our present moment is the only one on which we have a direct influx. The past is a lesson, and the future is an opportunity, but it is in the present that we can really act.

The weather is mysterious. We do not know what the future holds that is very well explained in the book on thinking about time by André Comte-Sponville. By understanding that the present, we must live our lives with intensity and perseverance, like Po in “Kung Fu Panda”. Remember that every day is a chance to become a better version of yourself. The present is our true power.
