Glossary

  1. Kung Fu Panda - A 2008 animated film directed by Mark Osborne and John Stevenson.
  2. Po - The main character of the movie, a clumsy panda who dreams of becoming a master of kung fu.
  3. Master Oogway - A character in the film who utters the memorable line "yesterday is behind, tomorrow is mystery and today is a gift, that’s why we call the present" and who teaches kung fu in Po.
  4. Perseverance - The quality of not giving up and continuing despite obstacles and failures.
  5. Animation - The technique of creating moving images through a series of drawings or models.
  6. Philosophy of time - Reflection on how human beings perceive and use time in their lives.
  7. Kung Fu - A Chinese martial art characterized by fast movements and specific fighting techniques.
  8. Animation cinema - The art of creating films by moving still images to give the impression of movement.
  9. Directors - Those responsible for the creation and direction of a film.
  10. Harmony - The state of concordance, balance and cohesion between different parts or elements of a work or team.
