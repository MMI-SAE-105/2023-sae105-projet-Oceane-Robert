Glossaire

  • Kung Fu Panda - A 2008 animated film directed by Mark Osborne and John Stevenson.
  • Po - The main character of the movie, a clumsy panda who dreams of becoming a master of kung fu.
  • Master Oogway - A character in the film who utters the memorable line "yesterday is behind, tomorrow is mystery and today is a gift, that’s why we call the present" and who teaches kung fu in Po.
  • Perseverance - The quality of not giving up and continuing despite obstacles and failures.
  • Animation - The technique of creating moving images through a series of drawings or models.
  • Philosophy of time - Reflection on how human beings perceive and use time in their lives.
  • Kung Fu - A Chinese martial art characterized by fast movements and specific fighting techniques.
  • Animation cinema - The art of creating films by moving still images to give the impression of movement.
  • Directors - Those responsible for the creation and direction of a film.
  • Harmony - The state of concordance, balance and cohesion between different parts or elements of a work or team.
