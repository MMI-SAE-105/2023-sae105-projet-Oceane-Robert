About

Welcome to our 'Replica' project! I'm Océane Robert, a first-year student at BUT MMI in Montbéliard, and I'm passionate about the power of quotes and memorable lines. Our aim is to create a captivating website around this theme, combining the world of time and the martial art of Kung Fu, inspired both by my personal line and the film 'Kung Fu Panda'.

Our group is made up of a variety of talents, each of whom has chosen a film and a replica that reflect their inspiration. Here's a glimpse of my friends' projects (feel free to click on the first name to see their projects):

  • Bisch Benjamin : “Dobby is free"
  • Collin Hugo : "The School of Sorcery"
  • Grosjean Romain : "Life Finds a Way"
  • Hashimoto Edouard : “Magic library"
  • Lallemand Maxence : "Endless Rebirth"
  • Malande Samba Claude : "Kulture"
  • Poilâne Mélissa : “Where we go, we don’t need a road”
  • Schmitt Ethan : "ShermanLegends"

Each project brings its own vision and creativity, forming a varied and dynamic web of replicas and interpretations of time, Kung Fu and much more. Our ambition is to offer you an immersive and inspiring experience through these different perspectives. Stay tuned to discover the magic of replicas and the art of storytelling on our dedicated website. We're delighted to share this adventure with you and hope you find it inspiring and thought-provoking.

Welcome to our world of replicas and exploration!

Océane Robert MMI 1 - Group A1
